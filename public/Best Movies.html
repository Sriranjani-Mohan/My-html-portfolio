My favourite list of Tamil movies

The best of 2023

  1. Vaathi

    2. Genre: Drama

    This movie talks about a man pursuing his dream of becoming a teacher. Circumstances forces him to move to a remote village to teach in a Government school. The story is about how he overcomes all the obstacles and help the children of the village to learn and pass their examinations.

  2. Maaveeran

    3. Genre: Fantasy

    This Movie is about a man living in slums of Chennai. He doesnt like confrontations or violence. His funda is to tolerate everything and live quietly but circumstances transcends him into a person of valour.

  3. Viduthalai:Part1

    4. Genre: Crime thriller

    This movie talks about how power in the wrong hands ushers chaos and commitment of attrocities in the society. This in return has consequences of the masses rising against the power in rebellion and a battle ensues.
